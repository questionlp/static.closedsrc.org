TBTL: Sounds and Ringtones

From this page, you can download various Too Beautiful To Live sound drops, clips and ringtones for use on your smartphone.

Note: If you are using an iPhone or iPad, you will have to download the "MPEG-4 Ringtone" version from your computer and use iTunes to upload them to your iOS device. For additional instructions, check out "Method Two of Three: Installing Third-Party Ringtones".
